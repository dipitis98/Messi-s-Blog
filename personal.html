Family:

Lionel Messi is married to Antonela Roccuzzo, his childhood sweetheart from Rosario, Argentina. They tied the knot in 2017 and have three sons: Thiago (2012), Mateo (2015), and Ciro (2018). Despite his fame, Messi is known to be a devoted family man who enjoys spending quality time at home.

Charity & Social Work:

Messi runs the Leo Messi Foundation, which supports children in areas like healthcare, education, and sports. The foundation has funded hospitals, research projects, and educational programs worldwide. Messi also contributes to UNICEF and often participates in charity matches and fundraising events.

Personal Interests & Lifestyle:

Unlike many football superstars, Messi lives a quiet and humble lifestyle. He avoids controversy and prefers spending time with his family. Outside football, he enjoys watching basketball, playing video games, and relaxing at home. His discipline in training and healthy lifestyle have helped him stay at the top for nearly two decades.
