The PSG Era (2021–2023)

Joining Paris Saint-Germain:

In August 2021, Messi signed with Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) after Barcelona’s financial crisis prevented a contract renewal. His arrival in Paris created global excitement, as he reunited with former teammate Neymar and joined forces with Kylian Mbappé.

Adapting to a New Club:

Messi’s first season in Ligue 1 was challenging. He struggled to settle in, managing only 6 league goals in 2021–22. Critics questioned whether he could adapt outside Barcelona, but he showed flashes of brilliance with assists and Champions League performances.

Return to Form:

In the 2022–23 season, Messi bounced back:

  • Scored 16 Ligue 1 goals and provided 16 assists.
  • Helped PSG secure back-to-back Ligue 1 titles.
  • Formed a dangerous partnership with Mbappé, contributing to one of Europe’s most feared attacks.

International Highlight:

During his PSG stint, Messi led Argentina to 2022 FIFA World Cup glory in Qatar — the crowning moment of his career. Though it was an international achievement, it defined his PSG years as well.

Departure:

By summer 2023, Messi decided to leave PSG, seeking a new adventure in the United States with Inter Miami. His two-year stay in Paris was short, but it added Ligue 1 success and valuable experience to his legendary career.
