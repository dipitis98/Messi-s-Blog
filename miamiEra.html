Inter Miami & MLS Era (2023–Present)

A New Chapter in the USA

In July 2023, Messi shocked the football world by joining Inter Miami in Major League Soccer (MLS). His arrival instantly boosted the league’s popularity worldwide.

Immediate Impact:

Messi debuted in the Leagues Cup and transformed Inter Miami’s fortunes:

  • Scored 10 goals in 7 matches.
  • Led the team to its first-ever trophy — the Leagues Cup 2023.
  • Brought global attention to American soccer.

Ongoing Legacy

Messi continues to inspire young players in the United States while balancing his final playing years with family life and global influence. His MLS journey shows he’s more than a player — he’s a global ambassador of the sport.
